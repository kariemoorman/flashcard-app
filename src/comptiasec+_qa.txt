"What is the CIA triad in information security?", "Confidentiality, Integrity, Availability"
"What is the purpose of a firewall?", "To control incoming and outgoing network traffic based on predetermined security rules."
"Define two-factor authentication (2FA).", "A security process in which the user provides two different authentication factors to verify their identity."
"What is the difference between symmetric and asymmetric encryption?", "Symmetric encryption uses the same key for both encryption and decryption, while asymmetric encryption uses a pair of public and private keys."
"Explain the concept of a VPN (Virtual Private Network).", "A secure, encrypted connection between two networks or between an individual user and a network over the internet."
"What is social engineering in the context of security?", "Manipulating individuals to divulge confidential or personal information that may be used for fraudulent purposes."
"Define penetration testing.", "The practice of testing a computer system, network, or web application to find security vulnerabilities that could be exploited by attackers."
"What is the purpose of an Intrusion Detection System (IDS)?", "To monitor and analyze network or system activities for malicious activities or security policy violations."
"Explain the principle of least privilege in security.", "Providing individuals or systems with the minimum levels of access or permissions needed to perform their functions, limiting potential damage from accidental mishandling or malicious intent."
"What is a zero-day vulnerability?", "A security vulnerability that is discovered by hackers before the software vendor becomes aware of it."
